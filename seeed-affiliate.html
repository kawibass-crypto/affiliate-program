FAQ

1. Commission Policies ▼

Q: What promotion methods are prohibited? A: To ensure fairness, please note the following restrictions:

  • No PPC advertising bidding: Affiliate accounts using paid ads to promote links will be closed with unpaid commissions.
  • No personal purchases with affiliate links/discount codes: Orders from your personal account will be manually reviewed, and we reserve the right to withhold commissions. Misuse of discount codes will result in immediate revocation without notice.

Q: How are commission rates structured and updated? A: Our commission system is designed to reward active affiliates:

  • Base Rate Guarantee: A 3% commission rate applies to all eligible products and will not decrease.
  • Tiered Rewards: High-performing affiliates may qualify for increased rates based on sales volume.
  • Seasonal Boosts: Quarterly limited-time campaigns may offer higher rates for specific categories.
  • New Product Launches: Exclusive short-term rate boosts for newly released products.

❗ All updates (rate changes, campaigns, etc.) will be notified via email.

2. Withdrawal Process ▼

Q: How do I withdraw my commissions? A: The minimum withdrawal amount is $50:

  • Go to "My Balance" in your account and click "Withdraw";
  • Email an electronic invoice matching the withdrawal amount to order@seeed.io
  • Payments are processed quarterly (March/June/September/December).

❗ Tip: Submit requests by mid-quarter (e.g., March 15) to expedite processing.

Q: Are there withdrawal timing restrictions? A: You may apply anytime, but payments are batched quarterly. Automatic payments are unavailable (platform limitation).

3. Orders & Invoices ▼

Q: Will I earn commissions if a referred order is cancelled? A: No. Commissions apply only to completed orders. Cancelled orders void associated commissions.

Q: Can you provide an Invoice Template? A: Yes! Download the template here and edit the yellow highlighted fields with your details.
